01 — The Origin

We started because mid-market companies deserved better.

In 2003, our founders saw a gap nobody was filling. Enterprise vendors built bloated platforms for Fortune 500s. Freelancers built basic websites. Mid-market companies — the ones doing $10M to $500M in revenue with real operational complexity — had nowhere to turn for software that could actually transform how they worked.

We built Azul Arc to serve them. Two developers, one project manager, and a simple philosophy: treat every client's problem as our own, understand the business before writing a single line of code, and never disappear after launch. Two decades and 200+ shipped projects later, that philosophy hasn't changed.
