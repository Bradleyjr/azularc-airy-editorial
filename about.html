Our Story

We started because mid-market companies deserved better.

In 2003, our founders saw a gap that nobody was filling. Enterprise software vendors built bloated, expensive platforms for Fortune 500 companies. Freelancers and small agencies built websites and basic apps. But mid-market companies -- the ones doing $10M to $500M in revenue, with real operational complexity -- had nowhere to turn for the kind of custom software that could actually transform how they worked.

So we built a company to serve them. We started with two developers, a project manager, and a simple philosophy: treat every client's problem like it was our own problem, understand their business before writing a single line of code, and never disappear after launch.

Over two decades, that philosophy has turned into 200+ shipped projects across government courts, manufacturing floors, and growing companies that needed systems as ambitious as their plans. Our team has grown, our technology has evolved, but the core commitment hasn't changed: we build software that works as hard as the people who use it.

Today, we're a team of designers, engineers, and strategists headquartered in Atlanta, with collaborators across the country. Our clients come to us because they've outgrown what got them here. They stay because we stick around long after go-live.
