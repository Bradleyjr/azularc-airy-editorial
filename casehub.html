Why CaseHub

Built for the way courts actually work.

Most case management systems were designed for a world where paper was the primary medium. The mandates have changed. The public's expectations have changed. The systems haven't.

CaseHub eliminates bottlenecks and enhances productivity. It simplifies collaboration among judges, clerks, and staff, keeping everyone on the same page without spreadsheets, endless emails, or paper trails.

Designed so well, it feels effortless. From initial filing to final disposition, every action is tracked with full transparency for compliance, oversight, and accountability.

Core Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

End-to-End eFiling

Submit, receive, and manage electronic filings in one secure system, eliminating paper backlogs and duplicate processes.

Role-Based Access & Permissions

Judges, clerks, attorneys, and staff only see what they need to, ensuring both security and efficiency.

Automated Notifications

From hearing reminders to filing confirmations, CaseHub keeps everyone updated with real-time alerts and calendar sync.

Comprehensive Audit Trails

Every action is tracked, giving courts and agencies full transparency for compliance, oversight, and accountability.

Multi-Case Type Support

Support for various case types ensures that CaseHub meets the unique requirements of every jurisdiction.
