Our Process

No black boxes.

A transparent, repeatable process honed over 200+ projects and 20 years. You'll always know what we're building, why we're building it, and exactly where things stand. No vanishing acts. No surprises at the invoice.

The Process

Five phases. Zero guesswork.

01

Discovery

Weeks 1-2

Before we write a line of code, we need to understand your world. Not just the technical landscape, but the people, the processes, and the pain.

group

Stakeholder Interviews

We talk to everyone who touches the system. Executives, managers, end users, IT staff. We learn what works, what doesn't, and what everyone wishes existed.

fact_check

System Audit

A full inventory of your current tech stack, integrations, data flows, and technical debt. We document what you have so we can build on what works and replace what doesn't.

map

Pain Point Mapping

We quantify every workaround, every bottleneck, every manual process. Each one gets a cost estimate so we can prioritize by business impact, not gut feeling.

02

Blueprint

Weeks 3-4

Discovery findings become a concrete plan. Architecture decisions, scope definitions, and a timeline you can actually hold us to.

architecture

Architecture Planning

Technology selection, system design, data modeling, and integration planning. We document the technical decisions and the reasoning behind each one.

target

Scope Definition

A detailed feature list, prioritized by business impact. Clear boundaries on what's in v1 and what's planned for later phases. No ambiguity about what you're getting.

payments

Timeline & Budget

A fixed-price estimate broken down by sprint and feature. You'll know exactly what each piece costs and when it will be delivered. No open-ended time-and-materials billing.

03

Design & Build

Weeks 5-16

This is where it gets real. Two-week sprints, working software at every milestone, and a feedback loop that keeps the product aligned with your actual needs.

sprint

2-Week Sprints

Each sprint has a defined scope, a clear deliverable, and a demo at the end. You see working software every two weeks, not a big reveal at the end of six months.

tv

Bi-Weekly Demos

Every other week, we show you what we've built. You test it, give feedback, and we adjust. This isn't a formality. It's how we ensure we're building the right thing.

autorenew

Iterative Refinement

Requirements evolve. We expect that. Our process is built to absorb changes without blowing up the timeline or budget, as long as we're making informed tradeoffs together.

04

Deploy

Weeks 17-20

Launch is not the finish line. It's the most critical phase. We handle migration, training, and rollout with the same rigor we bring to development.

database

Data Migration

We move your data from old systems to new with full validation, rollback plans, and zero tolerance for data loss. We've migrated millions of records across dozens of projects.

school

Team Training

Hands-on training sessions for every user group, from front-line staff to administrators. We create documentation, video guides, and reference materials your team can use long after we leave.

rocket_launch

Staged Rollout

No big-bang launches. We roll out department by department, region by region. Each stage is monitored, validated, and stabilized before we move to the next.

05

Evolve

Ongoing

Software is never "done." We stay on to monitor performance, fix issues, and build the features you'll need as your business evolves.

monitoring

Monitoring

Proactive performance monitoring, error tracking, and uptime management. We catch problems before your users do and resolve them before they become business disruptions.

build

Maintenance

Security patches, dependency updates, infrastructure optimization, and bug fixes. The unglamorous work that keeps your system running smoothly and securely.

add_circle

Feature Additions

New capabilities informed by real usage data. We track how your team uses the system and prioritize enhancements based on actual behavior, not hypothetical requirements.

Why It Works

What makes our
process different.

code

Working software every 2 weeks

You never go more than two weeks without seeing real, working software. Not mockups. Not slide decks. Functional code that you can click through, test, and give feedback on. If something is heading in the wrong direction, we catch it early.

group

Same team start to finish

The people who run your discovery are the same people who write the code and deploy the system. No handoffs between "strategy teams" and "delivery teams." No re-explaining your business to a new group of developers every few months.

favorite

We stick around after launch

Most agencies ship the project and disappear. We stay on for monitoring, maintenance, and evolution. Your system gets better over time, informed by real usage data and your evolving business needs. We're a partner, not a vendor.
