The Problem

The growing pains nobody talks about

01

Spreadsheets Everywhere

Your company runs on spreadsheets that only one person understands. When that person is out, everything stops. Critical business logic lives in cell formulas, not in systems designed to scale.

02

Off-the-Shelf Tools Breaking

You've maxed out every SaaS plan. You're paying for five tools that each do 20% of what you need. The integrations between them are held together with Zapier and hope.

03

Manual Processes That Don't Scale

What took one person an hour at 50 customers now takes a team of three all day at 500. You're hiring to manage process, not to create value. The workarounds have workarounds.

04

Data Silos

Sales data lives in one system, operations in another, finance in a third. Getting a complete picture of your business requires someone to manually pull reports from five different tools and combine them in yet another spreadsheet.

Our Approach

The growth-stage playbook

Phase 1

Untangle

We audit every system, spreadsheet, and manual process your company runs on. We map the data flows, identify the bottlenecks, and quantify exactly how much time and money your current setup is costing you. No assumptions. Just evidence.

Phase 2

Prioritize

Not everything needs to be fixed at once. We rank every pain point by business impact and build a phased roadmap that starts delivering value in the first sprint. Quick wins first, then systematic improvements that compound over time.

Phase 3

Build & Replace

We build your custom platform in 2-week sprints with working demos at every milestone. Old systems get replaced incrementally. Your team never has to survive a "big bang" switchover. Every deployment is planned, tested, and reversible.

Phase 4

Scale

After launch, we stay on to monitor, optimize, and evolve the system as your business grows. New features get added based on real usage data. The platform grows with you instead of becoming the next thing you outgrow.
