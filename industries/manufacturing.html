The Problem

What's slowing manufacturers down

Disconnected ERP & CRM

Your ERP talks to nobody. Your CRM has its own version of the truth. Sales promises delivery dates the warehouse has never heard of, and finance reconciles it all in a spreadsheet at month-end.

Manual Order Entry

Orders come in by phone, email, fax, and portal. Someone re-keys every single one. Errors compound, shipments go wrong, and your best people spend their days doing data entry instead of solving problems.

No Real-Time Inventory

You find out something is out of stock after you've already promised it. Cycle counts take days. Nobody trusts the numbers in the system, so they walk the floor to check manually.

Spreadsheet-Driven Reporting

Your leadership team makes million-dollar decisions based on spreadsheets that took someone three days to compile. By the time the report is done, the data is already stale.
