Service

Case Management
Systems

Track every case. Automate the rest. We replace paper-based and legacy systems with modern platforms that handle filings, workflows, compliance, and citizen access.

The Problem

Legacy case systems weren't built for how courts work today.

Most court and government case management systems were built 15 to 20 years ago. They were designed for a world where paper was the primary medium and in-person filing was the only option. The mandates have changed. The public's expectations have changed. The systems haven't.

We build modern case management platforms from the ground up. Not off-the-shelf software with a government label slapped on it. Purpose-built systems designed around your jurisdiction's specific workflows, compliance requirements, and constituent needs.

Our platforms handle everything from initial filing to final disposition, with full audit trails, automated routing, and public-facing portals that let citizens access the system without setting foot in a courthouse.

Deliverables

What we build for you

01

Workflow Automation

Automated case routing, deadline tracking, notification systems, and escalation workflows. Cases move through the system based on rules, not on someone remembering to check a stack of files.

02

Document Management

Digital filing, automated document generation from templates, e-signatures, version control, and intelligent search. Every document tied to its case with a complete audit trail.

03

Citizen-Facing Portals

Mobile-first public portals where citizens can check case status, file documents, pay fees, and schedule hearings without visiting a courthouse. Accessible, secure, and designed for people who don't read legal jargon.

04

Compliance Dashboards

Real-time compliance monitoring that tracks state and federal mandate adherence automatically. Configurable alerts when deadlines approach. Audit-ready reports generated on demand, not in three days.

05

Multi-Jurisdiction Support

A single platform that handles different case types, fee structures, and procedural rules across multiple counties or jurisdictions. Shared infrastructure, jurisdiction-specific configuration.

06

Integration Layer

APIs and connectors for law enforcement databases, state reporting systems, payment processors, and existing legacy systems. Your case management platform talks to everything it needs to, automatically.
